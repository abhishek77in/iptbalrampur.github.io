DOEACC Affiliated Courses

O-LEVEL

Objective
To enable a student to acquire the knowledge pertaining to fundamentals of Information Technology(IT) in order to serve the IT industry better the DOEACC O-Level cource has been designed to develop the basic skills.
The career options available to a DOEACC O-Level qualifiers are:

  1. Junior Programmer
  2. EDP Assistant
  3. Web-Designer
  4. Lab Demonstrator
  5. Computer Teacher
  • Cource content:
    • IT tools & Bussiness systems
    • Internet & Web-design
    • Programming & Problem solving through C language
    • Electives:(one module out of the following three modules have to be chosen)
      1. Application of .NET technology
      2. Introduction to Multimedia
      3. Introduction to ICT Resources
    • Project Work
    • Practical (based on selected module)
  • Eligibility:
    • 10+2 or higher
  • Duration:
    • 1 year
  • Fee Structure:
    • Cource fee: Rs.13,800


CCC - Cerificate in Computer Concept

Objective
This cource is designed to aim at imparting a basic level appritiation programme for the common people. After completing the cource the student is able to use the computer for basic purposes like preparing his personal business letter, viewing information on internet(the web), sending e-mails, preparing his business presentation, playing games and etc. This allows a common or house wife to be also a part of computer users list. This helps the small business, house wives to maintain there small acount using the computers and enjoying the world of Information technology. This cource is more practical oriented.

  • Cource content:
    • Introduction to computer
    • Introduction to windows
    • Basic DOS Commands
    • Elements of Word Processing
    • Spreadsheets
    • Computer Communication & Internet
    • WWW and Web Browsers
    • E-mail
    • Making small presentations
  • Eligibility:
    • 10th pass
  • Duration:
    • 4 months
  • Fee Structure:
    • Cource fee: Rs.6,360
